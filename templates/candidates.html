Why Elect a leader

The first duty of a man is to think for himself.

Election is a mechanism by which people choose their representatives at regular intervals and change them if they wish to do so. They can choose who will form the student committee and take major decisions for them. They can choose a leader who shares the same ideology as them to improve their lives in school.

  • A vote gives a student a voice.
  • Every vote has a major direct impact on the activites held through out the year.
  • Simulates real life elections.

Elections provide a way for the students to choose their leaders. They do so by casting their vote in favour of the candidate whose views appeal to them. This ensures that the will of the students is reflected in the elected candidates.
